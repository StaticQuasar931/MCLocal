This hub loads local split builds and shows a clear progress message while files are being joined. Singleplayer and offline use are the safest choices at school.

How loading works

Larger versions are split into parts. The hub joins them in the browser and then launches them in the game area.

School network warning

Servers will likely not work at most schools. Many school networks block multiplayer traffic, WebSockets, or outside connections. Offline play and local worlds are much more likely to work.

Quality of life
