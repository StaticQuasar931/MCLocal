How this works

The launcher opens a real about:blank tab. This hub lives inside it, and when you launch a version the wrapper swaps over to the game.

School server warning

Servers likely will not work at most schools. Many school networks block multiplayer traffic, WebSockets, or outside connections. Offline play and local worlds are much more likely to work.
